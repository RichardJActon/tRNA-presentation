tRNA expression

  • tRNA genes act as insulators (Raab et al.¬†2011 EMBO [5])
  • Clustered with other tRNA genes in 3D (VanBortle et al.¬†2017 Genome Biol [6])
  • RNA polymerase III (Schramm & Hernandez 2002 Genes & Dev [7])
  • Internal Promoter Structure
  • Experimentally DNAm \(\uparrow\ \rightarrow\) tRNA Expression \(\downarrow\) Besser et al.¬†1990 FEBS letters [8]

tRNAs in Ageing

  • \(\downarrow\) Translation, \(\uparrow\) Longevity (Hansen et al.¬†2007 Aging Cell[9])
  • Ageing ~ TORC1 \(\dashv\) Maf1 \(\dashv\) polIII \(\dashv\) tRNA expression
  • \(\downarrow\) polIII, \(\uparrow\) Longevity (Filer et al.¬†2017 Nature [10])
  • Circulating tRNA halves modulated by ageing and calorie restriction (Dhahbi et al.¬†2013 BMC Genomics[11])
  • tsRNAs in stress response & Translational regulation
  • tRNA biology is intimately connected with many systems which modulate ageing

Human tRNAome

  • Up to 610 tRNA genes Chan 2009 NAR [12]
  • median length 73bp
  • <46kb, 0.002% of the Genome
  • 2nd most abundant RNA species, ~60 million/cell

Enrichment for DNA hypermethylation

MeDIP-seq limitations

  • Regional
    • Good Coverage but limited positional resolution
  • Imperfect methylation Quantitation (especially at low DNAm)

Neonate & Centenarian

  • Whole genome Bisulfite Sequencing (WGBS)
  • tRNA hypermethylation even between single individuals

Validation in 450k array

  • Good Methylation Quantitation
  • CpG specific, probes within tRNA genes
  • Covers 103 tRNAs, 7 of the 16 tRNAome significant
  • 5 of these show an increase (\(4.39\times10^{-4}\), N = 587)
  • Cell-type corrected

Isodecoders

  • tRNA with the same anticodon but different sequence elsewhere
  • Numbers increase with organismal complexity (Goodenbour et al.¬†2006 NAR [14])
  • Non-equivalent translational efficiency (Geslain et al.¬†2010 J Mol Biol [15])
